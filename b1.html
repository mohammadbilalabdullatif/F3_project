Rashq 4Pc - Bridal Dress

PKR 114,990

Collection Details

Ready to wear 4Pc formal dress

Style yourself in the utmost graceful work of art by choosing our striking ensemble for your big day. It is the embodiment of your traditions and aspirations, all woven into one spectacular masterpiece.

Choli: Beautifully designed organza choli in the shades of deep red features hand embroidery all over - on the front, back, and sleeves. Embellishments incorporate dabka and kundan work along its neckline and waistline. To elevate the whole look, fancy tassels are added at back neckline and green stone hangings along the hem of sleeves and waistline.

Lehnga: Paired with a heavily embroidered lehnga made from premium quality organza. Touched up with foil embroidered borders and intricate hand work at front. This cascading lehnga comes with an attached can can.

Dupatta: A deep red dupatta in net fabric decorated with a spread of sequins and hand work. Further adorned with organza embroidered borders at four sides displaying heavy hand work at the head side.

Pouch: Fully embellished pouch with a fancy handle and green stone hangings.
